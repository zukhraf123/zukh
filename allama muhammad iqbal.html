Dr. ALLAMA MUHAMMAD IQBAL

Sir Allama Muhammad Iqbal was a South Asian Islamic philosoper and a great poet.His poetry is considered to be among the greatest of the 20th century.His vision of a cultural and political ideal for the Muslims of British-ruled India is widely regarded as having animated the impulse for the Pakistan Movement.He is widely considered one of the most important and influential muslim thinkers and Western religious philosopers of the 20th century.
